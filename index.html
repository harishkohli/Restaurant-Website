About

Chandran Mess, often referred to simply as "Chandirasekar T" is a popular eating establishment known for its delicious South Indian cuisine. It's typically found in Tamil Nadu, India, particularly in cities like Chennai and Coimbatore. In terms of food, Chandran Mess specializes in authentic South Indian dishes, particularly those originating from Tamil Nadu cuisine. This includes a variety of vegetarian and non-vegetarian options, such as dosas, idlis, vadas, sambar, rasam, various rice dishes like biryani and pulao, as well as delectable seafood preparations like fish curry and prawn fry.
